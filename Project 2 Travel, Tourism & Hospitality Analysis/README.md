# PROJECT 2


## Hotel Booking Demand Analysis with Dynamic Pricing and Cancellation Prediction

This repository presents a collaborative project focused on the travel and hospitality industry, where revenue losses frequently occur due to inefficient pricing strategies and unexpected booking cancellations. Using the Hotel Booking Demand Dataset from Kaggle, our team conducts Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and develops predictive models to identify the major factors influencing cancellations (churn) and to analyze seasonal booking patterns. The project also evaluates how demand changes across different customer segments and time periods. By combining data analysis with machine learning techniques, we aim to generate insights that help hotels improve pricing decisions and minimize revenue loss.

### Objectives

● Determine the key factors that influence booking cancellations such as lead time, deposit type, and customer segment.

● Visualize booking trends and seasonal demand patterns.

● Examine pricing elasticity and simulate dynamic pricing approaches.

● Deliver actionable insights that help maximize Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR) and reduce cancellation rates.

### Key Features

● Well-structured and reproducible Jupyter Notebook workflows.

● Customer segmentation and churn analysis.

● Visualization of seasonal demand patterns and booking curves.

● Baseline predictive models including logistic regression and decision trees.

● Business performance metrics such as ADR (Average Daily Rate) and RevPAR for revenue evaluation.

### Team Collaboration

This project is carried out by a team of four members, each responsible for a specific component such as EDA, feature engineering, predictive modeling, visualization, and business insights. The team collaborates through Git version control to efficiently integrate individual contributions into a unified project.

### About

A collaborative data analytics project using a Kaggle dataset to study cancellation drivers, seasonal demand variations, and pricing elasticity. The project integrates EDA, predictive modeling, and business insights to support better revenue management and reduce customer churn in the hospitality sector. Additionally, the findings aim to support data-driven decision making for hotel management and revenue optimization strategies.
